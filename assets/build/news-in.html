What we did:

We programmed an online store with 1C integration on 1C-Bitrix, connected 1C. We did the primary stuffing, took the project for support.

A little about the client:

The official representative of the Blum hardware manufacturer in the Republic of Belarus. The leader in the development, production and sale of fittings for kitchen and cabinet furniture of the highest quality: lifting mechanisms, hinges, drawers, guides and systems of internal dividers.
